Blades of Glory

"They laughed at Louis Armstrong when he said he was gonna go to the moon. Now he's up there laughing at them." -Chazz Michael Michaels

Blades of Glory follows the dramatic journey of two skating rivals turned teammates, Chazz Michael Michaels and Jimmy MacElroy. Michaels and MacElroy fell from the top of the single skating world after their fight atop the podium on the world stage. Now they must find a way to work together to make skating history and take on the unbeatable pair of Stranz and Fairchild Van Waldenberg.

Release Date: March 30, 2007

Director: Josh Gordan

Rating: PG-13
